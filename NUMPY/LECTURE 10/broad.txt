THIS LECTURE WAS ABOUT BROADCASTING OF ARRAYS DURING ARITHMETIC ALGEBRIC OPERATIONS

Brodcasting Rules :

1️⃣ Compare shapes from right to left

When two arrays are used in an operation, their dimensions are compared from the last axis moving left.

2️⃣ Dimensions must satisfy one of these conditions

Two dimensions are compatible if:

They are equal, or

One of them is 1

3️⃣ Missing dimensions are treated as 1

If arrays have different numbers of dimensions, the smaller array is padded with 1s on the left.

4️⃣ Expansion during operation

The dimension with size 1 is stretched (broadcasted) to match the other dimension.

5️⃣ Otherwise → Error

If dimensions are not equal and none is 1, broadcasting fails with an error.
